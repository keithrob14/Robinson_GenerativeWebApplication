Sphere Scribbler

Description

Use the controls on the right to manipulate the generated sphere. The controls are listed below. Right click to reset the canvas. Press the 'down arrow' to download an SVG file of the canvas.

Controls:

  • Size- Control the size of the sphere, affects the drawing size of the sphere
    Green- Controls the amount of green hue in the sphere ('red' is at a constant 100 value)
    Blue    - Controls the amount of blue hue in the sphere ('red' is at a constant 100 value)
    strokeWeight- Controls the stroke weight of the sphere.
    rotatex- Rotates sphere on its x-axis.
    rotatey- Rotates sphere on its y-axis.
    rotatez- Rotates sphere on its z-axis.
    strokeShade- Controls the shade value of the spheres strokes.
    moveLR- Moves the sphere left and right, along the x-axis of the page.
    moveUD- Moves the sphere up and down, along the y-axis of the page.
